Reasons To Use Trongate

Reason #2 : Stability

Trongate gets improved constantly. However, the makers of Trongate take the viewpoint that it should be possible to update and improve web development frameworks without breaking them.

Trongate's approach differs from frameworks like Symfony - which offers paid certification that's tied to specific version numbers. Just to state the obvious, that business model has given the makers of Symfony a clear financial incentive to reversion, rewrite and ultimately break their own framework frequently.

It should come as no surprise that the PHP frameworks that come with the option paid certification are the most frequently rewritten and least stable frameworks of the entire PHP space.

Trongate handles framework updates in a manner that is innovative and unintrusive. The video below offers more details.

We Don't Sell Certificates!

To be clear: Trongate will never offer paid certification because doing so would impede our top priority of ensuring the stability of the framework.
